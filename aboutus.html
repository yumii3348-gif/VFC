About Us

We are dedicated to raising awareness, empowering communities, and driving meaningful change in the fight against poverty.

Who We Are

Voices for Change is a community-driven advocacy group born out of compassion and determination during the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic. What started in 2020 as a small effort to address the struggles of families living in poverty has grown into a united movement of people who believe in creating opportunities for others.

Our founder, Shyra Reign V. Josue, started this advocacy while she was still a student. Despite her young age, she recognized the urgent need to support families who lacked access to food, healthcare, and education, especially during the pandemic. With courage and determination, she brought people together, turning a small initiative into a growing organization that continues to inspire change.

Through her leadership, Voices for Change has connected with organizations, volunteers, and changemakers who share the same vision: to fight poverty, promote access to education, and extend healthcare and food security to those who need it most. We are not just an organization — we are a family of volunteers, dreamers, and doers who believe that small acts of kindness, when combined, can create lasting change.

Founder's Message

“Change begins with a single step, and no one is ever too young or too small to make a difference. I started Voices for Change with nothing but hope and determination, and today, I stand with a family of changemakers who prove that compassion is stronger than fear.”

— Shyra Reign V. Josue, Founder of Voices for Change

Our Values

We believe in compassion, collaboration, and empowerment. By working together, we can break cycles of poverty and bring hope to communities in need.
