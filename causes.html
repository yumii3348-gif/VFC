Causes and Effects of Poverty

Poverty affects millions of people and limits access to basic needs. Below are the major causes and effects of poverty.

Causes of Poverty

Lack of Education

People with limited schooling have fewer job opportunities.

Unemployment

No stable jobs means families struggle financially.

Low Wages

Some jobs do not pay enough to meet basic needs.

Effects of Poverty

Hunger

Families may not have enough food or proper nutrition.

Poor Health

Lack of medical care leads to sickness and weakness.

Homelessness

People may lose their homes or live in unsafe conditions.
