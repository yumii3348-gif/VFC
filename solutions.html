Actions and Solutions to Fight Poverty

There are many programs, organizations, and simple actions that help reduce poverty. Below are solutions that communities, governments, and individuals can do.

Education Programs

Providing free or affordable education helps people get better jobs and escape poverty. Many groups support school supplies, scholarships, and literacy programs.

Health Assistance

Health programs like free check-ups and vaccination drives prevent illnesses that keep families trapped in poverty.

Livelihood Training

Skills training in sewing, cooking, farming, or digital work helps people start small businesses or find new jobs.
